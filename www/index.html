Determine high-risk zones in R

R package highriskzone

The highriskzone-package provides tools to determine and evaluate high-risk zones
of unobserved events using point process methodology. There are three methods implemented:
the method of fixed radius, the quantile-based method and the intensity-based method.

The package is based on "Determining high-risk zones for unexploded World War II bombs
by using point process methodology" by Mahling et al. published in Journal of the Royal
Statistical Society: Series C (Applied Statistics).

In course of developing this package I wrote my  bachelor thesis .
