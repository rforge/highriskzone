Determine high-risk zones in R

R package osmar

The highriskzone-package provides tools to determine and evaluate high-risk zones of unobserved events using point process methodology. There are three methods implemented: the method of fixed radius, the quantile-based method and the intensity-based method.

The package is based on "Determining high-risk zones for unexploded World War II bombs by using point process methodology" by Mahling et al. published in Journal of the Royal Statistical Society: Series C (Applied Statistics) (see http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1467-9876.2012.01055.x/abstract).
